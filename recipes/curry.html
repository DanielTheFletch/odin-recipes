Gourmet Meat Curry

A hearty dish whose spices from Death Mountain complement the fine meats to restore a healthy amount of hearts. One of the richer meals found in Northern Hyrule.

As described in Hyrule:

"The high-quality meat used in this prized dish satisfies meat and curry lovers alike."

Minimum required ingredients:

  • Gourmet meat (x1)
  • Goron spice (x1)
  • Hylian rice (x1)
