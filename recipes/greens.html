Sunny Fried Wild Greens

Thinking about traveling to The Depths? This light but nutritious meal will restore hearts and cure some hearts from gloom damage. Any adventurer expecting to come into contact with the gloom brought about by The Upheaval should consider carrying this meal for safety.

As Described in Hyrule:

"Restores hearts damaged by gloom. A basic vegetable dish made by sauteing fresh wild plants."

Minimum required ingredients:

  • Sundelion (x1 or more)
  • Optionally add any of the following:
    • Sun pumpkin
    • Hyrule herb
    • Korok frond
