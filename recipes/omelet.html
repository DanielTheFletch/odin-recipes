Crab Omelet with Rice

At a glance:

This seaside dish combines savory crab meat with filling cooked rice to create an immaculate flavor. Although the recipe dates back to Hyrule's earliest days, it has now become a staple among residents of Lurelin Village.

As described in Hyrule:

"The fluffy crab legs pair perfectly with the rice for a truly scrumptious dish."

Required ingredients:

  • Crabs (x2, must be two different types)
  • Hylian rice (x1)
  • Bird egg (x1)
  • Rock salt (x1)
